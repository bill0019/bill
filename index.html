Work

w

This is a selection of my deeplearning development work. I've been involve in many different types of project.

It's a project about using deeplearning method to make a diagnosis of the prostate cancer. Labled pathological images are provided by Beijing Hospital. With a resolution of 10K*10k, those big slides are divided into 256*256 patches, and with a series data augmentation oprations, those patches consist of the data set. Under the fram of Tensorflow, resnet_V2_50 is used to train the model, and at this stage, drop-out strategy is applied to get rid of over-fit, and AdamOptimizer is used in back-propgation. The evaluation results show that, the model gain an accuracy rate of 90.2%, and a recall rate of 84.6%. In post process stage, a heatmap is generated for each test pathological image, which visually shows the tumor zone.
